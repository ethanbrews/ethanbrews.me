PDA CONTROL LOGIC
error = target − distance
P = error × Kp  (+, pulls toward target)
D = velocity × Kd  (+, opposes motion in both directions)
A = accel × Ka  (+, anticipates velocity buildup)
raw = P + D + A
fan = clamp(raw, 0, 1)
D damps velocity overshoot  ·  A anticipates thrust lag  ·  fans off when at target and still
